# Westbrook School Budget Validation Referendum

## What Is This?

All Westbrook voters — regardless of party — will vote whether to approve the Westbrook School Department's fiscal year 2027 (FY27) budget, covering the 2026–2027 school year.

This is a **validation referendum**, required under Maine law. The Westbrook City Council voted 6-0 on May 4, 2026 to send the budget to voters.

## What You're Voting On

A **"yes"** vote approves the budget at $57.2 million. A **"no"** vote rejects it, which would send it back to the School Board and City Council for revision.

## What's In the Budget

- The school department is the largest component of Westbrook property taxes
- Most of the increase is driven by health insurance premiums and teacher salary costs (statewide trend)
- The budget was reduced from an initial $58.6M/10% proposal

## How to Vote

The budget referendum is on the same ballot as the June 9 Primary. Vote at your regular polling place.
