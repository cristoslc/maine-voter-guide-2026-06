# Cape Elizabeth School Budget Validation Referendum

## What Is This?

All Cape Elizabeth voters — regardless of party — will vote whether to approve the Cape Elizabeth School Department's fiscal year 2027 (FY27) budget.

This is a **validation referendum**, required under Maine law. Cape Elizabeth has its own school department, separate from South Portland.

## What You're Voting On

A **"yes"** vote approves the FY27 budget. A **"no"** vote rejects it and sends it back to the School Board and Town Council for revision.

## How to Vote

The budget referendum is on the same ballot as the June 9 Primary. Vote at your regular polling place.
