# South Portland School Budget Validation Referendum — June 9, 2026

## What Is This?

All South Portland voters — regardless of party — will vote whether to approve the South Portland School Department's fiscal year 2027 (FY27) budget, covering the 2026–2027 school year.

This is a **validation referendum**, required under Maine law since 2007. The City Council voted unanimously on May 6, 2026 to send the budget to voters.

## What You're Voting On

| | |
|---|---|
| **Total Budget** | **$76.2 million** |
| **Council Approval** | Unanimous (May 6, 2026) |
| **Previous Year (FY26)** | **$73 million** — approved June 2025 by 61% of voters. 6%+ tax hike |
| **Year-over-Year Change** | ~$3.2 million increase (~4.4%)

A **"yes"** vote approves the budget at $76.2 million. A **"no"** vote rejects it, which would send it back to the School Board and City Council for revision. If rejected, state law provides for a re-vote process with revised figures.

## What's In the Budget

Based on Press Herald coverage (February 11, 2026) and the district budget process:

- The school department accounts for approximately **61% of South Portland property taxes**
- Roughly **80%** of the budget goes to wages and health care for staff
- The district's initial projection to maintain current staffing was nearly **$83 million** — the final $76.2 million represents reductions from that level
- The School Board discussed a potential **1% budget increase** for snowplowing, school resource officers, and contingency/fund balance
- The school **reconfiguration plan** was discussed and may be deferred to FY28

## Historical Context

Maine school budgets have been under pressure statewide in FY27 due to rising health insurance premiums and teacher salary costs, the Maine School Management Association reports. Budgets are up 5-10% year-over-year in many districts.

## How to Vote

The budget referendum is on the same ballot as the June 9 Primary. All polling places open 7 AM–8 PM. **Vote at your regular polling place**, not the Community Center (unlike past June budget elections).

Find your polling place: [southportland.gov/586/Polling-Locations](https://southportland.gov/586/Polling-Locations)
