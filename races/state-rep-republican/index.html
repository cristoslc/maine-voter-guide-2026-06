State Representative — Republican Primaries

You must choose a Republican primary ballot to vote in these races.

What a State Representative does: One of 151 representatives in Augusta. Votes on every state law and the state budget. Your closest elected official. Two-year term. South Portland is split into three districts.

Which district am I in?
