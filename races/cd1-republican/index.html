Congressional District 1 — Republican Primary

Two candidates vie for the Republican nomination. Winner faces incumbent Democrat Chellie Pingree (in office since 2009) in November.

What a US Representative does: Votes on federal laws and the federal budget. All spending bills originate in the House. Your representative can direct federal money to your district — for harbor dredging, base funding, infrastructure grants. Two-year term.

Context: CD1 is deeply Democratic — "very demographically Democrat-voting" and "it's hard to unseat an incumbent." Russell lost to Pingree in 2022 by 95,000+ votes. "No Republican has cracked 37% in the last decade."

Who You'd Face

Incumbent Chellie Pingree (D) since 2009. Unopposed in Democratic primary. Won by comfortable margins against Russell in 2022. Mother of Hannah Pingree (gubernatorial candidate).

Ron Russell

Republican
Age 71 · Kennebunkport · Retired Army colonel, Airborne Ranger and Green Beret · Defense contractor · 2022 Republican nominee for CD1 · Ballotpedia

Platform

Economy & Taxes
Lower taxes and energy independence. Supports economic growth through deregulation and domestic energy production.
Maine Morning Star, May 22
Healthcare
Cut Medicaid waste and fraud. Supports the One Big Beautiful Bill (OBBB) including its Medicaid provisions. Opposes ACA premium subsidies. "The government should not be in the healthcare business."
Maine Morning Star, May 22
Immigration
Border wall and ICE cooperation. Law and order approach. Strong enforcement stance.
Maine Morning Star, May 22
Iran War
Said military campaign has "gone very well. It has been beneficial, and it will continue to be beneficial for the citizens of the United States, because we have been held hostage by Iran for 49 years."
Maine Morning Star, May 22
Ukraine
Supports aid but says it cannot be a blank check. Wants accountability for how funds are spent.
PBS debate
Trump
"I think the biggest thing is to provide support to President Trump and what he's trying to do." Would not be a "rubber stamp." When pushed for a disagreement, pointed to behavior rather than policy.
Maine Morning Star, May 26

Background

45 years of military and defense experience. Retired Army colonel, Airborne Ranger, Green Beret. Served in combat zones. Now a defense contractor. Ran against Chellie Pingree in 2022, losing by 95,000+ votes (roughly 37-60%).

Joshua Pietrowicz

Republican
Age 26 · New Gloucester · Sales · Political newcomer · Central Maine Christian Academy graduate · Ballotpedia

Platform

Economy & Taxes
Lower taxes on lower and middle class. Raise taxes on the ultra-wealthy. Increase domestic production. More manufacturing in the US.
Maine Morning Star, May 22
Healthcare
Supports a universal hybrid healthcare system — "basic safety net" for all, with private options layered on top. Supports ACA premium subsidies and opposes the OBBB's Medicaid cuts. More moderate than Russell.
Maine Morning Star, May 22
Iran
Opposes ground invasion. Supports naval blockade approach only. More cautious than Russell on military escalation.
PBS debate
Ukraine
Opposes continued aid. Says Zelenskyy must compromise.
PBS debate
Trump
"I don't like his style and a lot of things, and he's a very arrogant man. I do think his heart's in the right place as far as serving the nation goes." Called out the Qatar jet gift as a conflict.
Maine Morning Star, May 26
Civil Liberties
Pro-2A but supports some restrictions. Opposes assault weapons ban. Anti-censorship. Pro-religious freedom.
PBS debate

Background

Age 26. Sales professional. Political newcomer. Central Maine Christian Academy graduate. Offers "new energy, new blood" compared to Russell. Broke with standard Republican positions on healthcare (supports universal hybrid) and Trump (called him "arrogant").

Who You'd Face in November

Note: You vote in only one primary. The Democrat field is shown below so you can evaluate how this primary's candidates match up in the general election.

Chellie Pingree

Democrat
Since 2009 · North Haven · Unopposed in Democratic primary · Ballotpedia
Pingree has held this seat since 2009, winning by comfortable margins in a strongly Democratic district. CD1 voted for Harris by a significant margin in 2024. Russell lost to Pingree in 2022. Winning requires the Republican nominee to capture Democrats and independents — a steep challenge in this district.
