# Phase 3: Research Syntheses — June 2026 Primary

## US Senate (Democratic Primary)

**Race**: US Senator — Democratic primary to challenge Susan Collins (R-incumbent)

### Candidates

#### Graham Platner
- Oyster farmer, Marine Corps and Army veteran from Sullivan, ME
- Progressive insurgent; described as embodying "a quarter-century of raw American frustration" (Politico)
- Was "firmly in control" of the race after Janet Mills suspended her campaign (Maine Monitor, May 2026)
- Key positions: lower drug prices via government negotiation, federal minimum wage increase, tribal sovereignty for Wabanaki, rural hospital funding, anti-Iran war
- Website: grahamforsenate.com

#### David Costello
- From Brunswick, ME
- Limited press coverage found; appears to be a lower-profile candidate in the race

#### Janet Mills (SUSPENDED)
- Former Maine Governor (suspended campaign)
- Was called "establishment" candidate; primary described as "establishment vs. progressive wing" (Axios)

### Key Issues
- Defeating Susan Collins
- Healthcare/drug prices
- Rural hospital viability
- Iran war opposition
- Tribal sovereignty
