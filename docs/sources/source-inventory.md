## Gaps

1. **Candidate positions**: Official sources only provide names on the ballot, not candidate stances. All position research must come from campaign websites, debates, and other primary sources.
2. **School budget details**: The warrant PDF contains the financial statement but we need to analyze what the budget referendum proposes.
3. **Candidate contact info**: No central directory of campaign websites or emails. Must be collected individually.
4. **Precinct-to-polling-place mapping**: The voter lookup tool is an API, not a downloadable dataset. May need to compile manually.
5. **Ballot question text**: For November municipal ballot questions, text may not be finalized until after filing deadlines.
6. **Incumbent info**: Official candidate list doesn't mark incumbents. Must be cross-referenced from current officeholder lists.
7. **Photos**: No official candidate photo repository. Must collect from campaign sites or social media.
